About HalalVest
Shariah-compliant investing, demystified for Indian Muslims.

What is HalalVest?

HalalVest is a curated portfolio research platform for Muslim investors in India who want to grow wealth without compromising their faith. Every stock across our 18 portfolios is screened against AAOIFI Shariah principles — no banks, NBFCs, insurance, alcohol, tobacco, gambling, weapons, or adult content.

All return data is sourced from NSE/BSE official historical data and sector index benchmarks, verified and updated quarterly. Data as of April 2026. Next update: July 2026. ⚠️ This is for research and education only — not financial advice.

Data Methodology

Portfolio returns are weighted averages of top holdings, computed using split-adjusted NSE historical closing prices. CAGR is calculated as ((P_end / P_start)^(1/years)) − 1. Sector benchmarks are cross-referenced from NSE sectoral index data (Nifty IT, Nifty Pharma, Nifty Infrastructure, etc.) and verified against published fund fact sheets.

All data is hardcoded and manually verified every quarter by our research team. We use no live API feeds to ensure data accuracy and reliability. Website updates every quarter.

Halal Screening Criteria

🚫
Sector Exclusion
No banks, NBFCs, insurance, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, pork, gambling, or adult entertainment.
📊
Debt Ratio
Total interest-bearing debt ÷ total assets must be under 33%. Highly leveraged firms excluded.
💰
Interest Income
Non-compliant income (interest, haram revenue) must be below 5% of total revenue.
🔄
Quarterly Review
All holdings re-screened every quarter with updated financials from BSE filings.
🌙
AAOIFI Aligned
Methodology aligned with AAOIFI standards and Nifty Shariah Index guidelines (TASIS-screened).
