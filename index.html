What is HalalVest?
HalalVest is India's first comprehensive Shariah-compliant portfolio platform, offering 21 curated smallcases across the full risk spectrum. Every stock is rigorously screened against Islamic finance principles — no riba (interest), no haram industries, no excessive debt.

Our portfolios are designed for Muslim investors and ethical investors alike, covering conservative large-cap plays to high-growth small-cap opportunities, all while maintaining Shariah compliance.
🚫
Riba Exclusion
Zero exposure to conventional banks, NBFCs, insurance, and interest-bearing instruments.
📊
Debt Screening
Debt-to-asset ratio must be below 33%. High leverage companies are excluded.
💼
Business Purity
No alcohol, tobacco, weapons, gambling, pork, or entertainment industries.
📈
Income Purity
Interest income must be less than 5% of total revenues. Purification required otherwise.
🔄
Quarterly Review
All holdings re-screened every quarter to ensure continued Shariah compliance.
🌙
Scholar Certified
Screening methodology approved by certified Shariah finance scholars.
