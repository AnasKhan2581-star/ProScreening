About HalalVest
Shariah-compliant investing, demystified.

What is HalalVest?

HalalVest is a curated portfolio platform for Muslim investors in India who want to grow wealth without compromising their faith. Every single stock in our 21 portfolios is rigorously screened against Shariah principles.

We use real NSE price data from Yahoo Finance to show you actual historical performance — no made-up numbers. The SIP and lumpsum calculators use the real computed CAGR of each portfolio.

Halal Screening Criteria

Our five-step screening methodology:

🚫
Sector Exclusion
No banks, NBFCs, insurance, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, pork, gambling, or adult entertainment.
📊
Debt Ratio
Total debt ÷ total assets must be under 33%. Highly leveraged firms are excluded.
💰
Interest Income
Interest income must be below 5% of total revenue. Purification required otherwise.
🔄
Quarterly Review
All holdings re-screened every quarter with updated financials.
🌙
Scholar Reviewed
Methodology aligned with AAOIFI standards and reviewed by Islamic finance practitioners.

Data & Transparency

Charts and returns use real historical NSE price data fetched live from Yahoo Finance. Portfolio returns are computed as the weighted average of constituent stock returns. Where live data cannot be fetched (CORS or delisting), we show estimated figures based on sector benchmarks and clearly label them.

SIP calculations use the standard FV formula. Lumpsum uses compound growth. The CAGR used is derived from actual 3-year price data of the portfolio's stocks, not assumed.
