About HalalVest
Shariah-compliant investing, demystified.

What is HalalVest?

HalalVest is a curated portfolio platform for Muslim investors in India who want to grow wealth without compromising their faith. Every stock in our 21 portfolios is screened against Shariah principles — no banks, NBFCs, alcohol, tobacco, gambling, weapons, or adult content.

HalalVest fetches real adjusted close prices from Yahoo Finance on every session load. Returns are computed from the adjClose column, which accounts for stock splits, bonus issues, and dividends — giving you accurate, apples-to-apples historical performance.

How the data engine works

On load, HalalVest fetches 10Y monthly adjusted close data for all unique NSE tickers in the portfolios using Yahoo Finance's chart API (via a CORS proxy). CAGR for each period is computed as: ((P_end / P_start) ^ (1/years)) − 1, where prices are split and dividend adjusted.

Portfolio returns are a weighted average of individual stock returns, using allocation weights. Data is cached in sessionStorage so the fetch only runs once per tab. If Yahoo Finance is unavailable or rate-limits, research benchmark estimates are shown instead and clearly labelled.
